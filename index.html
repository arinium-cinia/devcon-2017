{ Theday outside thebox }

Cinia DEVcon 2017 is a meeting for all Cinia employees working in software. It is meant to be more than a technical get-together, where we look at the world outside Cinia.

We have three or four internal speakers who will talk about technologies and experiences outside their normal day-to-day routine at work. We also have an external keynote speaker who will enlighten us on the world of Blockchain and Bitcoin.

Demo Gallery in DEVcon is about your passion projects. We also have a full Virtual Reality setup too, so you can have a taste of the future.

{ Speakers }

Keir Finlow-Bates

Blockchain

Keir Finlow-Bates has been a Bitcoin and Blockchain enthusiast for years, and that passion turned into a full-time job when he founded the company Chainfrog. He has an MA in Mathematics and Engineering and a PhD in Mathematics and Education, and holds 19 patents in multiple technical fields.
/
In DEVcon Dr Finlow-Bates will give the keynote speech on his passion for Blockchain and Bitcoin.

15:00

Tuomas Piirainen

Full Stack Clojure Game Development

Introduction to web development with Clojure and ClojureScript using two game projects as examples. The first project is an overly ambitious 4X space strategy game that shows how not to do things. The other project is a web version of a simple board game which is more suited for exploring new technologies.

Topi Kanerva

Jython

They say that the Python language is way too old. But is it sneaky enough to fit into a large Java project? Find out how to make scripting easy by using Jython and how to benefit from the massive libraries of both Java and Python.
